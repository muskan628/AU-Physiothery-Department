Physiotherapy

Welcome to the Physiotherapy Outpatient Department (OPD) at Akal University (Department of Physiotherapy)
We are committed to serving our diverse community by offering all our services free of charge. This initiative reflects our mission to enhance accessibility to high-quality physiotherapy care for individuals from all walks of life, ensuring that everyone can receive the treatment they need.
Patient-Centric Approach
The design of our OPD prioritizes patient comfort and safety. Key features include:
  • Spacious Treatment Areas: Designed for optimal patient interaction, equipped with natural light and good ventilation to create a pleasant environment.
  • Efficient Reception Area: A well-organized space to facilitate smooth patient intake and management, minimizing wait times and ensuring a positive experience.
  • Private Treatment Spaces: Dedicated areas for confidential assessments and interventions, ensuring patient privacy and comfort.
Our Vision
At Akal University, we are dedicated to empowering individuals to reclaim their physical health and mobility. Our OPD is designed to provide comprehensive physiotherapy services, significantly impacting the quality of life for our patients.
