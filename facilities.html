Facilities

Our OPD is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and facilities to ensure the highest quality of care:
  • Electrotherapy Units: Advanced devices for muscle stimulation and pain relief, enabling effective treatment for various conditions.

  • Exercise Therapy Stations:A range of equipment, including multi-exercise stations, recumbent bikes, and resistance bands, designed to facilitate rehabilitation and fitness.

  • Gait Analysis Equipment:Specialized tools to assess walking patterns, providing insights that inform individualized treatment plans.
