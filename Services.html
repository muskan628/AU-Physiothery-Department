Services

Our Physiotherapist OPD provides a wide range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of diverse patient populations.Our primary focus includes:
Assessment and Diagnosis
  • We conduct through evaluations,including physical examinations and functional assessments, to identify specific conditions and limitations.This enables us to create targeted rehabilitation goals and strategies.

  • Taking assessments help evaluate a patient’s condition, identify impairments, and guide personalized treatment planning.
Therapeutic Modalities
  • Manual Therapy: Techniques such as joint mobilization, soft tissue manipulation, and myofascial release are employed to alleviate pain and enhance mobility.

  • Electrotherapy: Utilizing modalities such as Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation(TENS),ultrasound therapy, and interferential current for effective pain management and muscle stimulation.

  • Cryotherapy and Heat Therapy: Methods to reduce inflammation (cryotherapy) and enhance blood flow (heat therapy) to promote healing.

  • Heat therapy uses (e.g., hot packs)to relax muscles, improve circulation, alleviate pain, and promote tissue healing.
Exercise Therapy
  • Personalized exercise regimens are designed to improve strength, flexibility, and endurance. Our exercise programs may include:

  • Suspension Training: Utilizing body weight to enhance strength and stability.
  • Balance Equipment: Tools such as stability balls and balance boards to improve coordination and proprioception.
Specialized Rehabilitation Programs
  • Sports Rehabilitation: Tailored programs to facilitate recovery from sports-related injuries and enhance performance.

  • Neurological Rehabilitation: Comprehensive therapies for patients recovering from strokes, traumatic brain injuries, and neurological disorders, utilizing evidence-based practices for optimal outcomes.

  • Pediatric Physiotherapy: Specialized assessments and interventions for children with developmental disorders,ensuring age-appropriate care and support.
Post-Surgical Rehabilitation
  • Detailed rehabilitation plans for patients undergoing surgeries, focusing on restoring function and reducing recovery time.
Education and prevention
  • We provide education on ergonomics, injury prevention techniques, and wellness strategies to promote long-term health and prevent future injuries.
